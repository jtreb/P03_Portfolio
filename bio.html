Biography

Hi there! I am an aspiring 3D modeller currently studying at the School of Interactive Arts and Technology of Simon Fraser University. I discovered my passion for 3D modelling in 2016, when I enrolled into an animation course that introduced me to Autodesk Maya! The first time I saw the low-poly aesthetic and understood how “graspable” the skills required to make it, basically began my journey as a 3D modeller. From here on, I have been honing my skills in Maya, and applying these 3D assets in other programs such as Photoshop and Unity to create animations and games.
