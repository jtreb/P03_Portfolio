About Me

Who is Jameson?

Hi there! I am an aspiring 3D modeller currently studying at the School of Interactive Arts and Technology of Simon Fraser University. I discovered my passion for 3D modelling in 2016, when I enrolled into an animation course that introduced me to Autodesk Maya! The first time I saw the low-poly aesthetic in our studio lab, I immediately fell in love with the art-style, which led to 3D Modelling. This just inspired me to hone my skills in Maya, and applying these 3D assets in other programs such as Photoshop and Unity to create animations and assets for games.
