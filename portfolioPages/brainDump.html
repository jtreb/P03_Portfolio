The Brain Dump

What is this?

The Brain Dump is a place where some of my raw and un-polished artwork is placed! Think of this place as the reflection of how my mind works, and this also represents the "messy-ness" that happens before the professional and the publishing comes in. That being said, this also contains some artwork that I created in my own time for fun and practices. This is my way of saying, I'm human too and everything that comes with that.

Doodles

These doodles were all drawn and coloured in a mobile app called "Sketch". I would visit this app every time I was bored, procastinating, or even in-class. Sketch is my go-to app when I want to write down quick notes, doodle "curves" or draw characters. Much of these doodles were inspired from existing characters such as Zyra, Lissandra, and Evelynn from "League of Legends", or the leaders in "Duelyst". Although I am interested in 3D Modelling, one secondary goals is to become proficient in character concepts/creation. If I really like the character, I would draw them in Sketch.
